單句模式

This morning, I walk out of my hotel at seven. I feel hungry, so I look for breakfast nearby. A small shop is across the street. The entrance is open, and warm bread smells nice.

I read the menu by the door. The menu has eggs, toast, soup, and milk tea. I ask about the best meal there. The worker smiles and points at the counter. I order toast and a hot drink.

The fare is not high, so I feel relaxed. I pay with cash from my wallet. Then I wait near the window. A city route map is on the wall.

I look at the route after my meal. Soon, my food is ready and looks fresh. I eat slowly and enjoy the quiet room. This simple breakfast gives me good energy. I feel ready to explore the city again.
