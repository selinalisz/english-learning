On Saturday morning, Tom woke up late and decided to stay home instead of going out. He felt exhausted after a busy week at work. His weekend routine was simple — cook, clean, and rest.

First, he went to the kitchen and looked at the ingredients in the fridge. There were some eggs, cheese, and vegetables left. He made a simple omelette and put it on a tray to eat in the living room.

After breakfast, Tom decided to do the laundry. He put all his clothes in the washing machine and set the timer. Then he called his landlord about a broken faucet in the bathroom. "I'll come to fix it on Monday," the landlord said.

The rest of the afternoon, Tom read a book and listened to music. By evening, he felt much better. He smiled and thought, "A quiet day at home is the best kind of rest."
