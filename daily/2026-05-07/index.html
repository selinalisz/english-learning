單句模式

Mina arrives at the train station early. Her hotel room is not ready yet. She does not want to carry her bag. She looks for a locker near the entrance. A screen says, Lockers available on Level 1.

The fee is four dollars for one day. Mina checks the station map. Her route to the museum is simple. The ticket machine is beside the lockers. She buys a one-way ticket first. Then she puts her bag in a locker.

A worker asks, Do you need help? Mina says, Is this locker available? The worker says, Yes, pay the fee here. Mina smiles and walks to the platform. Now her hands are free for sightseeing.

Collocation

文章用法：Lockers available → 看英文告示時代表目前有空櫃。
beside the lockers
Fixed Expression
在置物櫃旁邊
文章用法：beside the lockers → 說明位置。
pay the fee
Collocation
支付費用
文章用法：pay the fee here → 在這裡付費。
one-way ticket
Collocation
單程票
文章用法：a one-way ticket → 買車票時常見說法。
Level 1
Fixed Expression
一樓、第一層
文章用法：on Level 1 → 看地圖或標示時判斷樓層。
for one day
Fixed Expression
一天、以一天計
文章用法：four dollars for one day → 費用是一天四美元。

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Is this locker available? 在車站、旅館、餐廳都能換字使用。看英文資訊時，available、fee、Level 1 這類字很實用，可以幫你快速判斷「有沒有、多少錢、在哪一層」。
