單句模式

Leo lands at the airport in the evening. He needs the hotel shuttle after his flight. He walks to a big sign outside. The sign shows each terminal and zone. Zone A is for Terminal 1. Zone B is for Terminal 2.

Leo checks the direction for Hotel Blue. It says, "Zone B. Wait by Door 4." He goes to Zone B quickly. A shuttle is there, but it looks full. Leo asks a staff member, "Is this shuttle for Terminal 2?" The staff member says, "No. This one goes to the city."

Leo looks at the sign again. A second shuttle stops by Door 4. The front sign says, "Hotel Blue." Leo smiles and says, "This is the right one." The driver says, "Good. We are ready now." Leo gets on and sits by the window.

🔗 Key Phrases
look at
Phrasal Verb
看；查看
看到標示、螢幕或地圖時很常用，是查資訊的基本動作。
get on
Phrasal Verb
上車
搭接駁車、公車或火車時都會用到，旅行口說很常見。
hotel shuttle
Collocation
飯店接駁車
到機場後常會先找這個關鍵字，能幫你快速判斷是不是自己的車。
Terminal 2
Collocation
第二航廈
航廈編號是旅行時最常先確認的資訊之一。
Zone B
Collocation
B 區
很多車站或機場會用 zone 分區，會看懂就不容易站錯地方。
Wait by Door 4
Fixed Expression
在 4 號門旁等候
這種句子很像你在標示或簡短指示上會直接看到的英文。
the right one
Fixed Expression
對的那一個
不確定有沒有搭對、排對或拿對時，這個說法很實用。
Is this shuttle for Terminal 2?
Fixed Expression
這台接駁車是去第二航廈的嗎？
今天最值得直接背起來的 survival sentence，現場確認搭車方向時可直接開口。

💡 Learning Tips
