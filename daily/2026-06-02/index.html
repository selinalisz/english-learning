單句模式

Mia has a doctor visit after work today. She checks her phone on the bus home. There is a new message from the doctor. It says, "Please come ten minutes early." The note is short, but very clear. Mia is not late this time.

She walks in and smiles at the receptionist. The receptionist says, "Hi, Mia." "Did the time change?" Mia asks. "No, but the doctor is busy," she says. "Excuse me, am I late?" Mia asks. "No, you are early," the receptionist says.

"Please wait five minutes." Mia sits down and drinks some water. She also checks the door number again. She reads the message one more time. Now she knows what to do. Short wait, clear message, and no stress today.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Excuse me, am I late?。看到預約訊息或 app 提醒時，先抓 new message、come early、five minutes 這種時間資訊，你會更快知道該不該立刻出門或再問一次。
