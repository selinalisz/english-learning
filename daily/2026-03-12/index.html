A First Night at the Hotel
📖 Today's Article

重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

Sarah arrived at the hotel after a long flight. She walked up to the front desk and handed over her ID. The receptionist smiled and said, "Welcome! We have your reservation. Would you like a room with a city view?"

Sarah nodded and asked about the breakfast time. "Breakfast is served from seven to ten in the morning," the receptionist replied. "You can also use the rooftop pool and the gym."

After getting her key card, Sarah took the elevator to the fifth floor. Her room was clean and comfortable. She opened the curtains and saw the bright city lights below.

She was exhausted from the trip, so she decided to order room service instead of going out for dinner. She looked at the menu, picked a simple meal, and called the front desk. Twenty minutes later, someone knocked on her door with a tray of hot food.

Sarah ate slowly and thought, "This is a great start to my trip."
