Finding the Airport Bus Bay

單句模式

Leo lands at the airport in the afternoon. He wants the express bus to Central Station. He checks a screen near the exit door. A small message says, "Express Bus, Bay 12." The hall is busy, so he looks for Bay 12 again. Then he walks outside with his bag.

Bay 10 is on the left. Bay 12 is not there. Leo asks a staff member, "Excuse me, is this Bay 12?" She says, "No, Bay 12 is upstairs." "Take the elevator and turn right," she adds. Leo thanks her and goes upstairs.

He sees a clear sign for the express bus. The sign says buses leave every twenty minutes. Leo waits a few minutes near the door. A driver smiles and opens the bus. "Does this bus go downtown?" Leo asks. "Yes, get on here," the driver says. Now Leo knows the right bay.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Does this bus go downtown?。看現場資訊時，先抓 Express Bus、Bay 12、every twenty minutes 這三種關鍵字，你會更快判斷自己該去哪裡等車。
