單句模式

Mia is out on an errand at lunch. She stops at a small noodle shop. A big sign is by the door. It shows today's lunch combo and drink. The combo has rice, chicken, and soup. A small note says, "Sauce is spicy."

Another note says, "Tell us about any allergy." Mia reads the sign two times. She wants the combo, but not the sauce. At the counter, she asks, "Can I get this combo without sauce?" The worker says, "Yes, no problem." Mia points at the card payment screen.

It says, "Please tap your card." She pays and gets ticket 12. The screen says, "Pickup section B." Mia waits by Section B and smiles. Soon the worker calls her number. She picks up her lunch and leaves.
