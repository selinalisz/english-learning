單句模式

The knock came again. Mina froze by the gate. Blue light moved under the door. Lina stayed outside the gate. Mina stayed inside the gate. Then a man spoke through the door. His voice was low and calm. "Did you get my messages?" he asked.

Mina held the paper ticket tight. She knew that voice. It was the blue bus driver. Lina whispered from outside, "Mina?" The driver said, "Step back, please." Mina took one small step. The bus door opened outside. No one else got off.

The driver slid a note under the gate. Mina picked it up fast. The note had the same date. "Tomorrow," it said again. At the bottom was one new line. "Seat 12 is not for you." Mina looked up at the door. "Why did you help me?" she asked. The driver answered, "Because she could not come back."
