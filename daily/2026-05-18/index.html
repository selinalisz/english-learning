單句模式

Nina has a package at a convenience store. Her store app says, "Ready for pickup today." It also shows a pickup code. The front door is closed today. A sign says, "Use the side door." Nina walks in and waits in line.

At the pickup desk, she shows her phone. "I am here to pick up a package," she says. The clerk asks, "Do you have a code?" "Yes," Nina says. "Can you scan this code?" The clerk scans the phone.

He checks a shelf and finds a small box. Nina checks the name on it. It is her package. She says thank you and puts it in her bag. Now she knows how to read the app and ask for help.

🔗 Key Phrases
ready for pickup
Fixed Expression
可以取貨了
文章用法：Ready for pickup today → 看 app 提示時很常見。
pickup code
Collocation
取貨碼
文章用法：shows a pickup code → 取貨時要出示的代碼。
side door
Collocation
側門
文章用法：Use the side door → 看告示時要判斷入口。
wait in line
Fixed Expression
排隊等候
文章用法：waits in line → 在店裡或車站排隊。
show her phone
Collocation
出示手機
文章用法：shows her phone → 出示 app、票券或取貨碼。
pick up
Phrasal Verb
領取、拿取
文章用法：pick up a package → 到店裡取包裹。
ask for help
Fixed Expression
尋求幫忙
文章用法：ask for help → 不確定流程時可以開口求助。

Lv.1 重組＋填空
「你可以幫我保管包包到今晚嗎？」

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：I am here to pick up a package. 到店取貨或旅行領物時可以直接套用。看英文 app 提示時，ready for pickup 和 pickup code 是關鍵線索，先找狀態，再找代碼。
