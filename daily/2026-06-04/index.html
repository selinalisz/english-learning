單句模式

Mia orders lunch at a food court. She wants the chicken special today. She asks for a mild sauce, not spicy. The cashier gives her a small ticket. It says, "Wait in Queue B." Mia looks at the pickup screen.

She waits for her number near Queue B. Many people stand near Section A. Queue B is on the other side. Mia walks there with her drink. A staff member smiles at her. Mia asks, "Excuse me, is my order ready?"

He says, "Not yet. Please wait here." Mia checks the screen again. Now her number is under Ready. It also says, "Pick up in Section B." Mia goes to the counter and picks up lunch. She shows her ticket and says thanks. Mia is happy she read the screen right.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Excuse me, is my order ready?。看英文資訊時，先抓 Ready、Queue B、Section B 這種關鍵字，你會更快找到正確的取餐位置。
