A Train Trip to the City
📖 Today's Article

複習單字
重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

I went on a train trip last weekend. I had a simple itinerary for the day. I wanted to visit a museum and a park.

I had a small snack on the train. It was a sandwich and some apple juice.

I arrived at the station at nine. Near the door, I picked up a brochure. The brochure had a map and helpful tips. It was easy to find the museum.

On the way home, there was a delay. I had to wait on the platform. The train was thirty minutes late. I felt tired, but I had a great day.

💡 Learning Tips
旅遊時，記得留意車站廣播。聽到 "delay"（誤點）或 "platform change"（月台更換）這些關鍵字，能幫你快速應對突發狀況。下次搭車時，也可以練習說 "Which platform does the train leave from?"（火車從哪個月台出發？）
