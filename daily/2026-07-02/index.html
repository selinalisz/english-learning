單句模式

Mina did not move. The driver waited outside the gate. Lina pressed her hand on the wall. "Who is she?" Mina asked. The driver answered, "May Lin." His voice stayed calm. Mina said, "Tell me the truth." The driver looked through the small window.

"You came one day early," he said. "Tomorrow was for May Lin." Mina held the note inside the gate. Lina whispered, "I cannot see him." Mina asked, "Can Lina come with me?" The driver said no at once. "May Lin rode alone," he said. "You must not make her mistake."

Mina looked toward the bus. The bus was empty inside the bus. No light was on in Seat 12. Then Lina stepped back fast. "Someone is behind me," she said. Mina turned to the gate. The driver was silent now. Only the bus door was open. A second voice said, "Mina, do not get on."

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接記的是 Tell me the truth.，當你想請對方直接說明實情時可以立刻用。今天也要記住 one day early，因為看日期、車票、預約時，提早或延後一天常常就是關鍵。
