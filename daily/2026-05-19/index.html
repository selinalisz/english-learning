單句模式

Eva is at the busy airport gate today. She checks her phone before her flight. Her app shows seat 24C in row 24. It is an aisle seat. Eva wants a window seat today. She wants to look out the window.

The app says, ask at the gate counter. Eva walks there and waits in line. "Hi, can I change to a window seat?" The kind woman smiles and takes her phone. She scans the code on the screen. "Let me check," she says.

A seat in row 21 is open. "It is 21A, by the window." Eva says, "Yes, that is great." The woman gives her the new seat. Now she can read the app and ask more clearly.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Can I change to a window seat? 到機場櫃台或登機門都能直接用。看英文資訊時，先抓 seat、row、counter 這些關鍵字，就比較快知道要去哪裡、要問什麼。
