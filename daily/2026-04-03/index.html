重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

Today is my day off. I wake up at eight. I feel happy and relaxed.

First, I tidy my room. I put things in the right place. It does not take long.

Then I make a simple meal. I eat bread and eggs. I drink some juice. The juice is cold and sweet.

After eating, I take my vitamin. It is a small habit. I do it every day.

In the afternoon, I sweep the floor. The room looks clean now. I put my pillow back on the bed.

I feel a little tired. So I take a short nap. When I wake up, I feel much better.

I love quiet days at home. They help me feel calm and happy.

💡 Learning Tips
學「家事」類動詞時，試著在做家事時用英文說出動作，例如掃地說 "I'm sweeping the floor"，洗碗說 "I'm washing the dishes"，這樣邊做邊說，記憶效果最好！

「take a nap」和「have a nap」都很常用，意思相同，都是「小睡一下」。遇到一個動詞搭配，也可以試試另一個動詞，看看哪個更順口。
