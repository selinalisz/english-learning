單句模式

Mina heard the bus stop outside the gate. Lina sent one more text. "The bus is here," she wrote. Mina looked at the screen again. The result was gone. The route history was gone too. In its place was one short line. "Check the last ticket," it said.

Mina opened the blue folder fast. A paper ticket fell out. The paper was thin, but clean. Mina turned it over. The date said tomorrow. Mina whispered, "Is this ticket for tomorrow?" Lina called right away. "Do you see a date?" she asked. "Yes," Mina said. "It says tomorrow."

Lina went quiet for one second. "Bring it to the gate," she said. Mina held the ticket by the record. The time and seat matched. At the bottom was one more line. "Seat 12. One rider only." Blue light moved across the wall. Then someone knocked on the gate.
