單句模式

Mina found the note at the bottom of her coffee receipt. Rain tapped on the cafe window. She came in to use the Wi-Fi code. Her phone was low, so she wanted an outlet. A small sign said, "No refill after six." Mina ordered hot tea and sat by the window.

Then she looked down at the receipt. A printed line was under the price. It said, "Don't take the blue bus tonight." Mina read it twice. It felt like a warning, not a mistake. Across the street, a blue bus stopped.

No one got off. The driver looked straight at the cafe. Mina held the receipt tighter. She asked the worker, "Who printed this message?" The worker looked at the paper. His face changed before he answered.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Who printed this message?。看收據、票券或 app 訊息時，先抓 printed message、warning、take the bus 這類線索，故事會更好懂，也更貼近真實情境。
