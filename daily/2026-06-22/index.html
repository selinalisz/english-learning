單句模式

Mina did not wait for the blue bus. The worker opened the side door for her. "Go now." he said in a low voice. She left the cafe with the receipt in hand. The old warning was still on it. Stop B was nearby, just one block away. A gray bus came before the blue one.

Mina asked, "Does this bus stop at River Street?" The driver said, "Yes. Get on now." She got on and sat near the front. The bus was quiet, but one empty seat was open. A small card was on that seat. The name used her first letters. It said, "This seat is saved for M.L."

Mina had not told anyone her plan. The man next to her kept reading his phone. The blue bus was still across the street. Then the card turned over by itself. One more line said, "Do not miss your ride." Mina stood as the bus started to move.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Does this bus stop at River Street?。上車前先用這句確認方向；看到座位卡、票券或 app 提示時，也要特別留意 saved for 和 Do not miss your ride. 這種提醒班次與座位資訊的線索。
