單句模式

Mina checks out at ten. Her train is at four. She does not want to carry her bag all day. In the hotel lobby, she sees a small sign. It says, "Bag hold until 9 p.m."

She walks to the front desk. "Can you hold my bag until tonight?" she asks. "Yes," the clerk says. "Please write your name on this tag." Mina writes down her name and phone number. The clerk puts the tag on her bag.

He gives her a card. "Show this card when you come back," he says. Mina asks, "What time does the desk close?" "We are here all night," he says. After arrival at the station, she feels light. She can walk, eat, and look around easily. Now she knows the sign and the words to ask.

look around

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Can you hold my bag until tonight? 這句在旅館寄放行李時可以直接開口。看英文資訊時，sign、bag hold、all night 這幾個線索很重要，先讀懂規則與時間，再決定能不能放心離開。
