單句模式

Ken stops at a pharmacy after work. He needs to pick up medicine for his mom. The screen says, Take a number. Ken takes number 18 and waits in line.

A sign says, Pay at the counter. Ken looks at his phone order. The pickup time is 6:30. The screen shows number 18. Ken walks to the counter.

The worker asks, Do you have a receipt? Ken says, Yes, here it is. He asks, Can I pick up my medicine here? The worker says, Yes, please wait one minute.

Ken pays and puts the medicine in his bag. He reads the name before he leaves. At home, he gives the bag to Mom. Mom says, Thank you, that was easy.

Phrasal Verb

pick up
Phrasal Verb

文章用法：pick up medicine → 領藥、取藥。
take a number
Fixed Expression
抽號碼牌、拿號碼
文章用法：Take a number. → 看英文提示時代表先拿號碼。
waits in line
Fixed Expression
排隊等候
文章用法：waits in line → 排隊等。
pay at the counter
Collocation
在櫃檯付款
文章用法：Pay at the counter. → 在櫃檯付款。
pickup time
Collocation
取貨或取餐時間
文章用法：pickup time is 6:30 → 取件時間是六點半。
here it is
Fixed Expression
在這裡、給你
文章用法：Yes, here it is. → 回應對方要看東西時很常用。

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Can I pick up my medicine here? 在藥局、櫃檯、取貨處都能改字套用。看英文資訊時，Take a number、pickup time、Pay at the counter 很實用，可以幫你判斷下一步要做什麼。
