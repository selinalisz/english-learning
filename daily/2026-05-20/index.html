單句模式

Mia goes to the laundry room after work. She wants clean clothes for tomorrow. She reads the signs on the machines first. One sign says, Card only. Another sign says, add detergent before you start. Mia finds an empty machine near the door.

She puts in one small load of clothes. Then she asks, "Does this machine take a card?" The man at the desk says, "Yes, it does." "The dryer still takes coins," he adds. Mia starts the wash and waits nearby. She checks her phone and drinks some water.

Later, she moves her clothes to the dryer. She still has enough coins in her bag. She sits by the window and waits. Soon the dryer stops, and her clothes feel warm. Now she can read the room faster. She also knows what to ask next time.

🔗 Key Phrases
after work
Fixed Expression
下班後
文章用法：goes to the laundry room after work → 描述日常安排很常用。
card only
Fixed Expression
僅收卡片付款
文章用法：sign says, Card only → 看告示或付款規則時很常見。
add detergent
Collocation
加入洗衣精
文章用法：add detergent before you start → 看步驟說明時要看懂。
put in
Phrasal Verb
放入
文章用法：puts in one small load → 日常放東西進機器或袋子都能用。
take a card
Collocation
可以刷卡；收卡付款
文章用法：Does this machine take a card? → 付費前先確認最實用。
wait nearby
Collocation
在附近等
文章用法：waits nearby → 等機器、等朋友、等取餐都可用。

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Does this machine take a card? 這句在洗衣店、停車場、自助機都很好用。看英文資訊時，先抓住 Card only 和 add detergent 這種短提示，你會更快知道規則和下一步。
