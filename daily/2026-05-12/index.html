單句模式

Leo wants lunch near his office. He checks a cafe website on his phone. The page shows today's hours: 11 to 8. It also says, "Lunch starts at 11:30." Leo looks at his work schedule. He has time before his next meeting.

At 11:40, he walks to the cafe. The cafe is crowded, but the line moves fast. A worker says, "Please take a number." Leo asks, "Is there a seat available for two?" The worker says, "Yes, table five is open." Leo waits near the front door.

Soon, he and his friend sit down. They look at the lunch set menu. They order soup, rice, and tea. Leo is happy he checked the hours first. It saves time and makes lunch easy.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Is there a seat available for two? 外出吃飯、等朋友或找位置時都能直接用。看英文資訊時，hours 和 Lunch starts at 11:30 這類時間提示能幫你先判斷店有沒有開、什麼時候去最剛好。
