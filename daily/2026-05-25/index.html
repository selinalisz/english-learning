單句模式

Mia buys lunch near her office today. The small cafe is crowded at noon. She opens the cafe app before she goes in. The app says, "Lunch coupon, good today." Mia looks for the pickup counter. A cashier smiles and asks, "Are you ready?"

Mia says, "Can I use this coupon?" The cashier says, "Yes, please show the code." Mia shows the code on her phone. The total price is 120 dollars. With the coupon, the total is 90 dollars. Mia pays for her lunch by card.

She waits in line near the door. The cashier says, "Your meal is ready." Mia picks up the bag and checks the receipt. The receipt also says, "Coupon used." Now Mia knows how to save money at lunch.

🔗 Key Phrases
look for
Phrasal Verb
尋找
文章用法：looks for the pickup counter → 找取餐櫃台。
pick up
Phrasal Verb
取、拿、領取
取餐、取貨、領包裹都很常用。
pay for
Phrasal Verb
付款、支付
買東西或點餐時可直接套用。
lunch coupon
Collocation
午餐優惠券
看 app 或店家活動時很常看到 coupon 搭配餐點。
pickup counter
Collocation
取餐櫃台、取貨櫃台
外帶、網購取貨、機場服務都可能看到。
total price
Collocation
總價、總金額
結帳畫面常見，用來確認最後要付多少。
in line
Fixed Expression
在排隊
排隊點餐、搭車、買票都能用。
good today
Fixed Expression
今天可用、今天有效
簡單看懂優惠券或活動資訊是否今天能用。
