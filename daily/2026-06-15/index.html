單句模式

Tina walks into a small cafe after work. The shop is busy, but one seat is open. She wants to send a message to her friend. A sign is on the wall by the door. It says, "Wi-Fi code at the counter." Another line says, "One outlet by the back seat."

Tina reads the sign for a minute. She also sees, "No refill on iced tea." The sign is short, but very clear. Tina goes to the counter and smiles. She asks, "Can I use the outlet here?" The worker says, "Yes, the seat in back is fine."

Tina pays with her card and gets a drink. Then she asks for the Wi-Fi code. The worker points at a small paper note. Tina sits down and plugs in her phone. Soon she sends the message and checks a map. Now she can rest and plan her next stop.
