重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

Today is a sunny morning. I wake up at seven. I go to the kitchen and have breakfast.

After breakfast, I do my first chore. I wash the dishes and clean the sink. I put a fresh towel in the bathroom. The bathroom looks clean and neat. Then I tidy my room. I put my books on the shelf. Everything is in the right place. I open the window and let in some air.

In the afternoon, I feel a little tired. I take a nap on the sofa. I sleep for thirty minutes. After my nap, I feel much better. I grab a snack from the kitchen. I sit outside and enjoy the nice weather. It is a simple day at home.

💡 Learning Tips
每次做完家務時，試著用英文說出你做的事，例如：「I washed the dishes.」、「I tidied my room.」邊行動邊說出口，記憶效果更好！另外，「take a nap」這類固定搭配（Fixed Expression）比單字更實用，背起來後可以直接套用在日常對話中。
