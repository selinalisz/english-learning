單句模式

Mia is at a small train station. She wants to visit the old town. The screen shows the blue line. It also shows the next train at 9:20.

Mia looks for a ticket machine. The machine says, Cash only today. She has cash, but no small coins. She walks to the counter.

She says, Can I buy a ticket here? The worker says, Yes, one ticket to Old Town? Mia says, Yes, please. The worker asks, One-way or return? Mia says, One-way, please.

She gets her ticket and checks the platform. The worker says, Platform two, train at 9:20. Mia thanks him and follows the blue line.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Can I buy a ticket here? 在車站、景點、售票櫃檯都能用。看英文資訊時，ticket machine、Cash only、one-way 這幾個說法很重要，能幫你判斷買票方式和票種。
