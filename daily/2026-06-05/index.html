單句模式

Mina lands in Osaka this afternoon. She follows the signs to baggage claim. Many people walk into the arrivals hall. Mina looks at a big screen there. It shows flight numbers and belt numbers. Her flight is a little late today.

She stands by Belt 6 first. A man says, "This is the wrong belt." Mina checks the screen again. She sees Flight 215 on the list. It says, "Belt 8." She walks across the hall quickly.

There are many people near Belt 8. Mina asks, "Excuse me, is this the right belt?" A staff member says, "Yes. Please wait here." Soon her blue bag comes around. Mina picks up her bag and smiles. Now she can leave the airport.
