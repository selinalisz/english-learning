單句模式

Mina stayed in the front seat. She looked at the card again. The words were still there. "This seat is saved for M.L." The bus ride felt too quiet. Mina took out her phone. She sent Lina a short message.

"Can you search Blue Route M.L. for me?" Lina replied at once. "I found a local post," she wrote. Mina opened the link. The title said, "M.L. missed the last blue bus." Under it was a photo of the same bus. It was written last year by May Lin. Mina read the post two times.

She asked the driver, "Should I get off at River Street?" He said, "One more stop." Then Lina wrote, "Wait. The post is changing." The title became, "Mina, get off at River Street." The next stop sign turned on. The bus slowed near a dark corner. Mina held her phone and stood up.
