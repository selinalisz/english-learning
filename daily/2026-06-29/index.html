單句模式

Mina came back early the next evening. She carried the blue folder and key. The folder felt heavy in her hand. The side gate was already open. The basement room was dark this time. She used the small key on a desk drawer. Inside was an old phone and a note. The note said, "Search Route 8:10 now."

Mina turned on the phone. The screen was slow, but it worked. She opened the browser and typed May Lin Route 8:10. No new message came at first. The first result showed a local bus forum. Mina whispered, "Is this the right result?" Then the title changed. "Do not trust the last map."

A second line appeared under it. "Check the route history before 8:10." Mina quickly opened the page. She saw the same blue bus photo again. One map did not match the bus record. Mina texted Lina, "Can you see the gate?" Lina replied, "Yes. I'm outside. I hear a bus coming." Mina looked at the clock. It was 8:07.
