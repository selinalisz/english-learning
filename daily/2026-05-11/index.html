單句模式

Mei is in a new city. Her itinerary says, "Take bus 12 to Central Market." She opens a map app. The map shows two bus stops. The app says, "Use Stop B."

Mei looks for a blue sign. A driver waits near the bus. Mei asks, "Does this bus go to Central Market?" The driver says, "Yes, get on here." Mei taps her ticket card. A front seat is available. She sits near the front.

The screen says, "Next stop: Central Market." Mei checks the bus number again. The driver says, "Get off after this bridge." Mei says, "Thank you, I will get off here." She arrives on time for lunch.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Does this bus go to Central Market? 在旅行時確認公車、捷運或接駁車都很好用。看英文資訊時，bus stop、Stop B、Next stop 這類字眼可以幫你快速判斷要在哪裡上車或下車。
