單句模式

Lina stops at a supermarket after work. She wants fruit and a sandwich. Her basket is small, so a fast line helps. A blue sign points to the self-checkout line. It says the line is for small orders. One machine is available, but one is closed.

Another screen says, "Card only. No cash." Lina has cash in her wallet today. She walks to the counter and asks for help. "Can I pay in cash here?" she asks. The worker smiles and points to another checkout. "This line takes cash," he says.

Lina checks the price on the small screen. She pays, and the machine gives a receipt. The worker says, "Please keep the receipt." Lina can check out fast and go home.

small orders
Collocation
少量購物、小筆購買
有些快速通道會限定 small orders only。

Collocation

買東西、搭車、旅途中都很實用。
keep the receipt
Collocation

退換貨、報帳、確認金額時很常聽到。
card only
Fixed Expression
只收刷卡
看到這句就要知道不能付現，也很適合拿來搜尋付款規則。
ask for help
Fixed Expression
請求幫忙
不確定流程時，這是最安全的開口方式之一。
after work
Fixed Expression
下班後
描述日常安排與生活節奏時很好用。

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Can I pay in cash here?。如果你在商店、車站或旅途中看到 card only，就要立刻知道不能付現；而 price 和 keep the receipt 很適合拿來看付款畫面或搜尋付款規則。
