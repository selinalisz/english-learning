A Bus Ride to the Park

重要片語

單句模式

On Saturday, I want to visit a new park. I pack water, a snack, and my phone. My friend cannot come, so I travel alone. I walk to the bus stop near my corner. A small sign shows the bus route.

I check the route right away. Then I wait under a tree. Soon, the bus comes and the driver smiles. I ask about the fare for the park. He says it is thirty dollars.

I tap my card and find a seat. A guide on my phone helps me. It says the main entrance is very big. I watch shops, bikes, and people outside.

After twenty minutes, I see the park gate. I get off and feel very happy. The trip is easy, calm, and fun. I want to take this bus again.

get off
