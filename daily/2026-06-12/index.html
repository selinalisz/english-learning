單句模式

Amy lands after a short trip. She walks into the arrivals hall. Many people are there, but the line moves fast. A big sign says "Passport Control." Another sign says, "Visa Check Here." Amy stops and reads both signs.

She has her passport in hand. She wants the right line, not the wrong one. A worker stands by the door. Amy asks, "Do I need a visa?" The worker says, "No, just show your passport." Amy goes to the desk and waits.

The officer looks at her photo and page number. He asks, "How many days will you stay?" Amy answers, "Five days." Then he gives her a stamp. Amy checks the date before she leaves. She asks, "Is the train this way?" The worker says, "Yes, follow the exit sign." Amy smiles and walks to the station.
