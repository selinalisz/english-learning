單句模式

Mina reached the bottom of the stairs. The small light stayed on. She was in a quiet basement room. It looked like an old storage room. A desk stood at the end of the hallway. On the desk was a blue folder. Mina picked up it with both hands.

Inside was a bus record. One page said, "Route 8:10 p.m." Another line had May Lin's name. Mina asked, "What time was the last bus?" No one answered, but a screen turned on. The screen showed the same blue bus. Then a new line appeared. "Please keep this safe."

Mina saw a small key under the folder. The old gate above was not really locked. Someone wanted her to find this room. Lina called, but the phone had no sound. The screen flashed one more message. "Come before 8:10 tomorrow." Mina held the folder and the key very tight.
