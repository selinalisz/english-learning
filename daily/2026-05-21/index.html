Checking the Train Board

單句模式

Amy is going to the airport by train today. She wants a direct train, not a slow one. At the station, she looks at the departure board. The board says, "Airport train, Track 4, 10:20." She checks the time again and walks to Track 4. Near the stairs, she sees the lockers are full.

Amy has one big bag and one small backpack. She goes to the information desk for help. "Can you hold my bag for two hours?" she asks. The woman says, "Yes. Please put this tag on it." Amy writes her name and phone number. Then she leaves the bag at the desk.

Before she leaves, she checks the departure board again. The train is still on time. A man asks, "Is this the direct train?" Amy says, "Yes, and this track is correct." Now she can get on the train faster. She also knows what to ask next time.

direct train
Collocation
直達車
文章用法：want a direct train → 找交通資訊時很常看到也很常說。
departure board
Collocation
出發看板
文章用法：look at the departure board → 看時間、月台、班次最實用。
hold my bag
Collocation
幫我暫放包包
文章用法：Can you hold my bag for two hours? → 寄放行李時可直接套用。
put on
Phrasal Verb
貼上；掛上；放上去
文章用法：put this tag on it → 看櫃台指示或行李流程時很常見。
on time
Fixed Expression
準時
文章用法：The train is still on time → 看班次資訊時要先懂這句。
get on
Phrasal Verb
上車
文章用法：get on the train faster → 搭車、搭船、搭巴士都會用到。

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Can you hold my bag for two hours? 到車站、旅館或景點寄放行李都能直接用。看英文資訊時，先抓住 departure board、Track 4、on time 這種短訊息，你會更快知道要去哪裡、班次有沒有變。
