单句模式

At 8:06, Mina stood at the gate. A blue bus was on the left. A gray bus was on the right. Her mission was clear: make a choice. She opened the old map. She opened the old phone too.

The screen showed route history. A red line said, "Check before 8:10." Another line said Seat 12 did not match. Mina asked a worker for help. She said, "Which bus goes to River Street?" The worker said, "Look at the screen first."

The screen gave two options. Mina used the map to compare names. One name did not match the old result. Mina decided to get on the safe option at 8:10. Lina texted, "Do not trust the first screen." Mina looked again, and the two options switched places.
