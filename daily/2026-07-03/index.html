Mina held the gate and listened.Lina stood by the wall outside."No one is here," Lina said.Then the old phone rang in the drawer.The voice came from the phone speaker."Do not get on with him," it said.The driver stayed by the bus door."You must choose now," he called.

Mina opened the blue folder again.A folded map fell from the back pocket.The paper felt cold and damp.A red circle marked Seat 12.Below it, she saw the letters M.L.Another name was near the gate.It was Mina Lin, in blue ink.Lina looked at the bus again.

"The driver is inside," she whispered.Mina looked through the small gate window.The empty seats did not match that voice."Can I check the map first?" Mina asked.She picked up the phone.The route history was still on the screen.Under it, one saved name said, "May Lin."The driver knocked on the gate again."The truth is on the map," he said.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是 Can I check the map first?，在看路線、看站牌、看景點地圖時都能直接用。今天也特別值得記住 on the screen 和 route history，因為你在 app、網站或看板上查資訊時很常會看到這種說法。
