Every morning, Li Mei follows the same routine. She wakes up at seven, makes a cup of coffee, and gets ready for work.

Her daily commute takes about thirty minutes by subway. She usually reads on her phone or listens to a podcast during the ride. It is a good way to start the day.

At lunchtime, she often runs a quick errand near her office. She stops at the grocery store to pick up fresh vegetables for dinner. Li Mei always checks her budget carefully before buying anything extra.

After work, she returns to her neighborhood and cooks at home. She is very efficient in the kitchen — dinner is usually ready in less than twenty minutes. Sometimes she makes extra food and saves the leftovers for the next day.

Before bed, Li Mei packs her bag and sets her alarm. It is a simple but satisfying life.

🔗 Key Phrases
get ready for work — Fixed Expression
準備上班
文章用法：gets ready for work → 整理好準備去上班，也可說 get ready for school / get ready for bed
run an errand — Phrasal Verb
跑腿、辦差事
文章用法：runs a quick errand → 辦個小差事，常用複數：run errands（跑好幾件事）
pick up — Phrasal Verb
順手取拿、順手買
文章用法：pick up fresh vegetables → 順手買些蔬菜，也可說 pick up a coffee / pick up the kids
check one's budget — Collocation
核查預算
文章用法：checks her budget carefully → 仔細核查預算，相關搭配：plan a budget、stick to a budget
save the leftovers — Collocation
保存剩菜
文章用法：saves the leftovers for the next day → 把剩菜留到隔天吃，也常說 eat the leftovers

💡 Learning Tips
今天的主題是城市日常生活。注意文章中描述時間順序的詞語：every morning、at lunchtime、after work、before bed，這些在日常對話和寫作中非常實用！另外，run an errand 和 pick up 這兩個動詞片語很常見，例如：I need to pick up some milk（我要去買牛奶）或 Can you run an errand for me?（你能幫我辦個事嗎？）多練習這些片語，你的口語會更自然。
