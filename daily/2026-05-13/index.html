單句模式

Mina lands in Osaka in the evening. After arrival, she checks her hotel email. It says, "Shuttle pickup is outside Exit 3." She follows the airport sign to the bus area. Many people wait in line there. Mina reads each hotel name slowly.

She asks a worker, "Is this the shuttle to Green Hotel?" The worker says, "Not this one." Please wait by the blue sign. Soon, a driver opens the door. Mina asks, "Does this shuttle go to Green Hotel?" The driver says, "Yes, it stops by the west lobby."

He checks her name on a list. Mina gets in with her small bag. The ride is short and quiet. At the hotel, the lobby is bright. Mina feels calm and ready to check in.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Does this shuttle go to Green Hotel? 抵達機場、車站後，只要不確定接駁車對不對，就能直接開口。看英文資訊時，outside Exit 3、pickup、lobby 這三個提示字，能幫你更快判斷集合點和下車位置。
