單句模式

Emma takes the subway to a new museum. She buys a ticket at a machine first. The ride is short and direct. At Pine Station, she looks for an exit map. One sign says, "Museum -> Exit B." Another sign says, "No elevator. Use the escalator."

Emma checks the map again before she walks. She follows the sign and goes up. Outside, she sees a small street with cafes. She is not sure, so she asks a worker. "Is Exit B for the museum?" she asks. "Yes, go straight and cross the street," he says.

"Thank you. Is it far?" "No, it's five minutes away," he says. Emma smiles and walks there with no problem. She does not need to ask many people. The signs help her find the right street.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Is Exit B for the museum?。看到 Exit B、go straight、cross the street 這類指示時，要把它們當成找路線的關鍵字；而 exit 和 escalator 很適合拿來看車站告示或搜尋站內路線。
