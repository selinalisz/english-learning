Reading the Station App
📖 Today's Article

複習單字
重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句
單句模式

Leo leaves work at six. He wants to meet a friend in town. At the station, he opens the train app. A message says, "West stairs closed today." He looks around and sees the same sign.

He needs a one-way ticket, so he walks to the machine. He says, "Where is Line 2 now?" A worker points left. "Use the east door," she says. Leo follows the sign and goes down slowly.

On the wall, he sees a map and the next train time. He buys his ticket and waits by the line. He checks the app again before he gets on. Soon, his train comes. Leo smiles. He can read the app and ask for help. He gets there on time.

🔗 Key Phrases
train app
Collocation
火車或捷運 app
文章用法：opens the train app → 先看站內資訊再行動。
one-way ticket
Collocation
單程票
文章用法：needs a one-way ticket → 買票時很常直接用到。
look around
Phrasal Verb
四處看、環顧
文章用法：looks around → 先比對 app 與現場標示。
go down
Phrasal Verb
往下走、下去
文章用法：goes down slowly → 看到改道後改走另一邊樓梯。
ask for help
Fixed Expression
請求幫助
文章用法：ask for help → 看不懂動線時可以直接開口。
on time
Fixed Expression
準時
文章用法：on time → 會看資訊就更容易準時到。

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Where is Line 2 now? 站內動線一變，你就能馬上開口問。看英文資訊時，train app 和 closed 這兩個線索很有用，先讀懂公告，再決定要走哪一邊。
