單句模式

Nina takes the bus to work every day. This morning, her card does not work. She goes to a ticket machine near the stop. A small screen says, "Card balance: 20 dollars." Nina looks at the red message again. It says, "Low balance. Please reload soon."

She joins a short queue and waits. In one minute, it is her turn. Nina presses the screen and reads, "Select payment." She can use cash or a bank card. She chooses cash and adds ten dollars. A man behind her asks, "Are you ready?"

Nina says, "Almost. I need one minute." He smiles and waits in the queue. The screen now says, "New balance: 30 dollars." Nina asks, "Can I reload this card here?" A worker says, "Yes. You are done now." Nina gets on the bus and smiles.

🔗 Key Phrases
look at
Phrasal Verb
看；查看
看到螢幕或告示時很常用，讀英文資訊前常先做這個動作。
get on
Phrasal Verb
上車
搭公車、火車、飛機都很常見，是旅行和通勤都要會的片語。
ticket machine
Collocation
售票機
車站常見設備名稱，看懂後比較容易自己完成加值或買票。
card balance
Collocation
卡片餘額
看交通卡、禮物卡、支付畫面時都可能看到這個資訊。
low balance
Collocation
餘額不足
當畫面顯示這句時，就表示很快需要加值或付款。
Select payment
Fixed Expression
選擇付款方式
在機器、網站或 app 上都很常見，是查資料時很值得認的介面英文。
one minute
Fixed Expression
等一下；一分鐘
需要對方稍等一下時，這個說法簡短又自然。
Can I reload this card here?
Fixed Expression
我可以在這裡加值這張卡嗎？
今天最值得直接背起來的 survival sentence，在車站或商店確認流程時很好用。
