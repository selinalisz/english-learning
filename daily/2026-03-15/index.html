Planning a trip is one of the most exciting things to do. First, I set a budget so I know how much money I can spend. Then, I look for flights and check the departure times online.

I always try to pack light because heavy luggage can slow you down at the airport. Before I leave, I always make sure my passport is in my bag. On the day of travel, I take the early bus to the airport.

I check in at the counter and then walk through security. After that, I find my gate and wait to board the plane. Sitting on the plane, I feel excited to explore a new place. Even when the journey feels long, the joy of arriving makes it all worth it.

At my destination, I like to walk around and explore the local neighborhood. I try local food, visit new places, and take many photos. Every trip is a chance to break your routine and see the world in a new way. I always come home feeling happy and ready for everyday life again.

💡 Learning Tips
💡 explore 的用法：「explore」後面可直接接名詞，例如 "explore a city"、"explore the neighborhood"。它比 "visit" 更有「深入探索」的意味，強調主動發現的過程。

💡 board 在機場廣播中的用法：機場常聽到 "Now boarding at gate 3."（現在在 3 號登機門登機）。注意 "board the plane" 和 "get on the plane" 意思相同，但 "board" 更正式。
