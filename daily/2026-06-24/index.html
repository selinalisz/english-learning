單句模式

Mina got off at River Street. The street was quiet and wet. One stop sign stood by the corner. A torn poster was on it. The poster had May Lin in an old photo. Below it was a bus map. Mina looked at her phone again.

Lina sent the same post title once more. Mina used search on the route name. A small note was under the map. It said, "This stop closed last year." Mina stepped back and looked around. Then she saw a blue arrow on the wall. It pointed to a narrow side street.

Lina sent one more message. "Look for the blue arrow." it said. Mina whispered, "Where is River Street Station?" An old man near the bench looked up. He said, "There is no station here now." Then he pointed at the same blue arrow. Mina held her breath and walked closer.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Where is River Street Station?。看站牌或舊公告時，先抓 bus map、This stop closed last year. 這種資訊句，它們常直接告訴你地點現在還能不能用。
