單句模式

Nora buys a blue shirt after work. At home, she notices a small mark. She checks the store website. The page says, Return policy: 7 days. It also says, Keep your receipt.

The next day, Nora goes back to the store. She takes a number and waits in line. A clerk stands at the service counter. Nora says, Can I exchange this shirt? The clerk asks, Do you want a refund? Nora says, No, I need a new size.

The clerk checks the shelf. One blue shirt is available. The exchange is free today. Nora pays no extra fee. She leaves with the right shirt.

service counter

right shirt
Collocation

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背的是：Can I exchange this shirt? 到店裡退換貨、買錯尺寸時都能直接用。看英文資訊時，return policy 和 refund 很實用，可以幫你判斷能不能退、能不能拿回錢。
