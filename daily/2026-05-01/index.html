Finding the Right Exit

單句模式

On Saturday morning, Mia arrives at a busy station. She wants to visit a small museum near the river. First, she checks the route on her phone. The app says, Take Exit B and walk left.

Mia looks at the big screen above the gate. It shows the train time and the fare. She buys a ticket and follows the blue line. At the platform, she hears a short message. Train to Central Park, platform three.

She gets on and takes a seat. Ten minutes later, she gets off at River Station. She looks for Exit B, but sees Exit A first. She asks a worker, Excuse me, where is Exit B?

The worker points to the left entrance. Mia says thanks and follows the sign. Soon, she sees the museum across the street.

💡 Learning Tips
