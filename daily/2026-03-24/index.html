A Quiet Day at Home
📖 Today's Article

重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

Saturday is my favorite day of the week. I wake up late and feel very happy. I make a meal for breakfast. I cook eggs and eat toast with butter.

After breakfast, I tidy my room. I put books on the shelf. The room looks clean and nice now. Then I remind myself to do the laundry. I put all my clothes in the machine and turn it on.

I wait and read a book on the sofa. The book is very good and I enjoy it. In the afternoon, I go for a walk. The air is fresh and the sky is blue. When I come home, I have a small snack. I eat some fruit and drink tea. It is a very nice and quiet day.

💡 Learning Tips
學日常生活英文最有效的方法，就是用英文描述你每天在做的事！試著每天說一句，例如 "I woke up at 7." 或 "I did the laundry today."，不需要很長，說得出來就是進步 💪
