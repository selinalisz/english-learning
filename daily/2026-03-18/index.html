A Calm Morning at Home

A Calm Morning at Home

重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

Every morning, I try to start the day well. I wake up at seven and turn off my alarm. Then I stretch for a few minutes to wake up my body. After that, I freshen up and go to the kitchen to make breakfast.

I prefer to eat a simple meal at home rather than buying food outside. It is cheaper and healthier. While I eat, I organize my plans for the day. I write down the things I need to do so I do not forget anything important.

Some mornings can feel stressful if I wake up late. But when I have a clear plan and follow a simple morning routine, everything feels calm and easy. I feel ready to face the day.

After breakfast, I wash the dishes and tidy up the kitchen. Then I pick up my bag and head out. A good morning makes the whole day better.

🔗 Key Phrases
wake up
醒來、叫醒
文章用法：wake up at seven → 七點醒來；wake up my body → 喚醒身體｜類型：Phrasal Verb
turn off
關掉、關閉
文章用法：turn off my alarm → 關掉鬧鐘｜類型：Phrasal Verb
freshen up
梳洗整理、振作精神
文章用法：freshen up → 梳洗整理儀容，準備出門｜類型：Phrasal Verb
morning routine
早晨日常規律
文章用法：follow a simple morning routine → 遵循簡單的早晨日常規律｜類型：Collocation
face the day
迎接這一天
文章用法：feel ready to face the day → 感覺準備好迎接新的一天｜類型：Fixed Expression
tidy up
整理、清理
文章用法：tidy up the kitchen → 整理廚房｜類型：Phrasal Verb
pick up
拿起、取
文章用法：pick up my bag → 拿起我的包｜類型：Phrasal Verb
head out
出門、出發
文章用法：head out → 出門去（通常指離開家）｜類型：Fixed Expression
