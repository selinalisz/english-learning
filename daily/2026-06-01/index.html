單句模式

Leo is at the airport this afternoon. He looks at the departure board near the cafe. His boarding pass says Gate 18. He has his passport in his hand. But the board shows a gate change now.

The new number is easy to miss at first. Leo stops and checks again. He walks to Gate 18 first. A worker says, "This gate is wrong for your flight." Leo stays calm and shows his boarding pass.

"Excuse me, did the gate change?" he asks. "Yes," she says. "Go to Gate 24." "Is the flight late?" Leo asks next. "No, it is on time," she says. Leo goes to the right gate right away. Now he can sit and read in peace.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Excuse me, did the gate change?。看到機場或車站資訊時，先抓 departure board、gate change、on time 這幾個關鍵詞，你會更快判斷自己要不要立刻移動或再確認。
