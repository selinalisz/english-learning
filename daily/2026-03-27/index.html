A Walk Through the Old Town
📖 Today's Article

重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

I arrived in the old town on a sunny morning. I had a map in my hand. The streets were small and very pretty.

I walked down a small path by the river. The path was quiet and clean. I stopped near a famous landmark. It was a very old stone bridge. Many people came to take photos there.

A local woman sold drinks near the bridge. I bought some cold water and sat down. Then I walked to the market. I wanted to buy a souvenir for my family. A friendly guide at the market helped me choose.

She showed me some nice bags and cups. I chose a small bag for my mom. I had a great time in the old town.

💡 Learning Tips
旅遊時，先在地圖（map）上找好地標（landmark），再沿著路徑（path）走過去。看到當地（local）的小攤販或市場，不要猶豫，進去逛逛！買個紀念品（souvenir）帶回家，旅途中遇到的每件小事都值得記憶。
