單句模式

Mia stops at a lunch shop at noon. She looks up at the big menu board. Today’s chicken special looks good and cheap. One red note says, "Sold out now." Another line says the curry is spicy.

Mia wants soup, rice, and tea. She checks her coupon before she gets in line. When it is her turn, she talks to the cashier. "Is the chicken special sold out?" she asks. "No, we still have it," he says. "Is it spicy?" Mia asks next. "No, it is mild," he says.

She orders the special and gives her coupon. The cashier shows the total on the screen. Mia pays and waits by the pickup desk. She can read the board and order fast.

💡 Learning Tips
今天最值得直接背起來的是：Is the chicken special sold out?。看到菜單板或 app 上的 sold out、special、on the screen，要把它們當成快速判斷資訊的關鍵字；而 spicy / mild 很適合在點餐前先確認。
