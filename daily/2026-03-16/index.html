A Busy Weekend at Home
📖 Today's Article

重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

Every weekend, I try to stay organized and get things done at home. On Saturday morning, I start by doing the laundry because clean clothes make me feel better. After that, I go to the supermarket to buy fresh ingredients for the week.

In the afternoon, I tidy up my room and organize my schedule for the coming week. Planning ahead can feel stressful at first, but it actually helps me stay calm. I write down all my appointments in a notebook so I do not forget anything.

In the evening, I like to sit on the sofa and read a book or watch a movie. A well-organized weekend makes the whole week feel more manageable. Taking care of everyday tasks may seem simple, but it really helps me stay happy and productive.
