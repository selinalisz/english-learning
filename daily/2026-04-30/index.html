單句模式

After dinner, I clean my room and wash my clothes. I carry a small bag to the laundry room. My white shirt and black pants look dirty today. First, I check each pocket very carefully.

I take out a tissue from one pocket. Then I put the clothes in the machine. I add blue detergent and close the door. The machine starts, and I sit nearby. I read a short message and drink water.

Soon, the clothes are clean and smell fresh. I move them to the dryer by the wall. I put one coin into the machine. Then I press the round start button.

While I wait, I fold my socks by hand. I also place a clean shirt in the drawer. The room is quiet, warm, and bright. After twenty minutes, I open the dryer. My clothes are dry, and my room feels tidy again.
