A Short Ferry Trip
📖 Today's Article
今日單字
複習單字
重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句

Last week, I took a ferry to a small island. The trip was only two hours. I brought my passport and one bag of luggage. I was very happy on the boat.

On the ferry, I looked at a map of the island. I wanted to visit many places. A kind man gave me a useful tip. He said the north beach is very beautiful. He also said the local food is great.

I had a fun day on the island. I walked on the beach and took many photos. I ate a delicious meal. The return trip was easy and calm. I felt very happy and relaxed. I want to go back again soon.

💡 Learning Tips
旅遊時，多問當地人「Can you give me a tip?」就能得到很多有用的建議！記憶 tip（建議）和 return（回程）時，可以想像自己搭渡輪回家，這樣的畫面更容易記住。
