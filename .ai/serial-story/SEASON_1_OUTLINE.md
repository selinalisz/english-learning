## Season 1 Arc

Mina discovers that English messages are appearing in places where they should not be: receipts, bus screens, cafe signs, station apps, and hotel notes. The messages first protect her from small problems, then reveal that someone is guiding her away from one specific blue bus route.

By the end of Season 1, Mina should learn that the messages are connected to an old route map and a person who disappeared after taking the last blue bus.

## Episode Guide

1. **The Note on the Receipt**
   Mina finds `Don't take the blue bus tonight` on a cafe receipt. The cafe worker reacts with fear.

2. **The Wrong Stop**
   Mina checks a bus app and sees the blue bus listed under the wrong stop. A small English notice says `Use Stop B after 8 p.m.`

3. **The Empty Seat**
   Mina boards a different bus. One seat has a card that says `This seat is saved for M.L.`

4. **Lina's Message**
   Mina tells Lina. Lina searches online and finds a local post about a blue bus that changed routes years ago.

5. **The Station Screen**
   At the station, the screen flashes `Delay: unknown reason`, then changes to Mina's name for one second.

6. **The Lost Phone**
   Mina finds a phone near the bus stop. Its lock screen shows a map with the cafe marked.

7. **The Hotel Desk**
   Mina asks a hotel desk worker about an old route. The worker says the route number is no longer used.

8. **The Search Result**
   Mina searches the route online. The first result title changes while she is reading it.

9. **The Last Ticket**
   An old ticket appears in Mina's bag. It has tomorrow's date, but the paper looks old.

10. **The Driver's Question**
   The blue bus driver finally speaks to Mina and asks, `Did you get my messages?`

11. **The Closed Platform**
   Mina follows a sign to a closed platform. A new message says `You are early.`

12. **The Name on the Map**
   Mina sees her initials on an old printed map, but the map is from before she arrived.

13. **The Choice at 8:10**
   Mina must choose between the safe route and the blue bus route at exactly 8:10 p.m.

14. **Season 1 Turn**
   Mina learns the sender is not trying to scare her. The messages are asking for help.

## Episode Design Notes

- Each episode should be readable alone, but it should also move the mystery forward.
- Every 3-4 episodes should answer one small question and open one bigger question.
- Keep the danger quiet and everyday: routes, wrong stops, missed buses, strange screens, unknown messages.
- The story should still feel useful for travel and daily English.
